# Use Cases

1. **Morning brief** — Daily digest of weather, calendar, meal prep reminders, birthdays, and urgent flags — delivered on demand via Telegram
2. **Email triage** — Monitors inbox on a schedule; surfaces what matters, flags time-sensitive items, drafts responses for approval
3. **Calendar management** — Reads across multiple calendars; creates OOO blocks, adds events, flags conflicts and upcoming commitments
4. **Meal planning** — Weekly dinner plan built around dietary preferences, freezer inventory, calendar, and health guidance from a paired nutrition agent
5. **Grocery list generation** — Organizes weekly shopping by store section; ready for same-day delivery order
6. **Outfit planning** — Weekly kid clothing guide based on weather forecast, school schedule, and activities
7. **CRM & relationship tracking** — Monitors birthdays, anniversaries, and grief dates; surfaces reminders at the right time without over-notifying
8. **Egg sales & invoicing** — Logs farm egg sales, tracks running balance, generates and sends monthly invoices via Gmail API
9. **Recurring household reminders** — Pet medications, seasonal tasks, school prep, activity kit checks — surfaced at the right time automatically
10. **Child schedule coordination** — Tracks kids' activities, school events, and logistics; surfaces what needs to happen and when without exposing child data outside the household
11. **Send-gated email** — All outbound email requires explicit passphrase approval; Heidi drafts, [OWNER] authorizes, nothing goes out automatically
12. **Multi-agent coordination** — Works alongside a paired nutrition/health agent; reads shared docs for cross-agent context without direct messaging
